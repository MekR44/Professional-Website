Professional Expertise

I am a 40 year veteran of the tech industry and first found my feet there when first generation computers were in vogue (to be found these days, in museums!).

In these years, I have guided the growth of several companies, including a start-up that I cofounded, through several eras of information processing and technology—from mainframes, client-servers, and web based solutions, to today's all-mobile offerings.

My journey has taken me to multiple continents, leading global teams and executing projects for international customers. I have worn many hats and worked with people from all walks of life.

In turn, this has brought me expertise in wide-ranging aspects of the business world, with a special focus on customer services and operational management of software companies with a distributed workforce.
